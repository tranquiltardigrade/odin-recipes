French Toast

Description

Quick and easy french toast recipe for a delicious morning breakfast. With just five ingredients you can make the best french toast you will ever taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices of bread

Directions

  1. Beat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with syrup, if desired.
  4. Voila, easy and delicious french toast!
